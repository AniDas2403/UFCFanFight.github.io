FAN FIGHT CARDS

Khabib Nurmagomedoc vs Justin Gaethje

The fight for the title. Khabib is the current undefeated, undisputed reigning lightweight UFC champion. After his last win over the "El Cucuy" or the boogeyman, the hitman of the division, Tony Ferguson, Justin Gaethje is riding high on confidence! But the question remains, will it be enough to acheive what other people have repeatedly failed , snatch the belt off Khabib's waist!

Tony Ferguson vs Connor McGregor

Another exciting fight, one man looking to spring back from his loss against THE HIGHLIGHT Justin Gaethje, which broke his 12 fight win streak, and another fresh back in the UFC, winning against THE COWBOY in a spectacular manner! Connor always has his left hand going for him, but Tony showed us his chin stregnth in the Gaethje fight.

Dan Hooker vs Dustin Poirier

Charles Oliveira vs Dan Hooker

Hooker's last fight was a win against Paul Felder, and Oliveira had his last loss against Felder. It just makes sense to have Hooker and Oliveira fight it out, perhaps followed by a Oliveira vs Felder 2 to determine the #5, #6 and #7 rankings for now.
