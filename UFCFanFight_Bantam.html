FAN FIGHT CARDS

Petr Yan vs Aljamain Sterling

Petr Yan is one of the deadliest fighters right now in the Bantamweight division, ranked at #3 and with a fight win streak of 6, unbeaten since his Octagon debut. The FUNK MASTER, the #2 contender, on the other hand has beun unbeaten since his loss against MAGIC Moraes in 2017. This will be an excellent match up to watch for the bantamweight title fight, now that the belt is up for grab after Cejudo's retirement.

Petr Yan vs Cody Garbrandt

Cody Garbrandt announced his return with the stunning second round buzzer knock out of the #5 ranked Raphael Assuncao. He wants his belt back but he would be facing a tough challenge in Petr Yan. This should be an exciting fight, with "NO LOVE" shown and "NO MERCY" given!

Cody Garbrandt vs Song Yadong

Both fighters are on coming ooff big victories in exciting fights and both would be looking to move forward. This would be a big fight for both of them and they would know it, and we can be sure that niether of the two would shy away from some wild exciting Octagon action.

Sean O' Malley vs Marlon Vera

Sean O' Malley promised us a SUGAR show, and boy has he delivered. Undefeated, with his latest knockout coming against Eddie Wineland, Sean O' Malley can easily challenge the ranked contenders and Marlon Vera might just be the perfect challenge for him right now. Coming off a loss against Song Yadong via decision in an adrenaline-filled fight, the CHITO would definitely set off fireworks in the SUGAR show, and we would love to watch that!
