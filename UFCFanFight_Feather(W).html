UFCFANFIGHT

MEET THE CHAMPIONS
Amanda Nunes vs Felicia Spencer

Result : Winner Amanda Nunes by decision

Amanda Nunes defended her Featherweight belt against Felicia Spencer, in UFC 250. Spencer definitely gave us her best performance, but in front of THE LIONESS, everything prooved to be futile, as Amanda had 132/222 strikes and 124/214 significant strikes, ascompared to Felicia's 70/161 and 42/130. Amanda had 6/7 succesful takedown attempts, compared to Spencer's 0/7. Amanda even had a submission attempt. We can all agree that Amanda has further stregnthened her grip on the featherweight division belt, and the entire UFC Women's division in general!

UFC 250, June 6 2020, Saturday
