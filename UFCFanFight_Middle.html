MEET THE CHAMPIONS

FAN FIGHT CARDS

Israel Adesanya vs Paulo Costa

Both have had an unbeaten record since their debut in the Octagon, and both have last fought against Yoel Romero and won, in vastly different methods. There has been a lot of heat going on between the two for some time, and THE LAST STYLEBENDER will have ample chances to show off his skills against THE ERASER in his third title defence, specially after Israel's muted fight against Yoel Romero.

Robert Whittaker vs Darren Till

The big boys fight, it would be fun to watch THE REAPER duel it out against THE GORILLA. Whittaker will be hunting to get back to his winninng ways while Darren Till will be high on confidence after wins over Masvidal and Gastelum

Edmen Shahbazyan vs Jared Cannonier

Edmen has been looking good, unbeaten since his Octagon debut, his last win coming off the number 11 ranked contender Brad Tavarez, and maybe it is time for him to face a top 5 ranked contender. Jared Cannonier who has found considerable success moving down to the middleweight from the light heaviweight, with victories over the legendary Anderson Silva and the sixth ranked contender Hermansson

Yoel Romero vs Jack Hermansson

After three losses against Whittaker, Costa and Adesanya, Yoel will be looking to show everyone just why he is know as the SOLDIER OF GOD. On the other hand, we all love to see THE JOKER in action, both in Gotham and inside the Octagon
